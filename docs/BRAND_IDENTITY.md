# Brand Identity — Karma by ReZ

## Core Brand Idea

> **"Good actions should be visible, valued, and rewarding."**

Karma is: **Positive · Human · Impact-driven · Rewarding**

It should feel: **Warm + Trustworthy + Modern** — not NGO-like, not corporate-heavy.

---

## Brand Personality

| Trait | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 🌱 Purposeful | Focused on real impact |
| 🤝 Human | Community-driven |
| ⚡ Rewarding | Instant value |
| 🔥 Motivating | Encourages action |
| 🧠 Smart | Tech-enabled, not charity-like |

---

## Brand Positioning

> **"The app where doing good actually gives you value."**

---

## Name Usage

- **Primary:** `Karma`
- **Secondary:** `Karma by ReZ` (for trust & ecosystem link)

---

## Logo Direction

### 1. Circular Flow *(Most Important)*

Represents: Action → Reward → Repeat

Visual: Circular arrows, loop symbol, infinity-style motion

### 2. K + Impact Symbol

Letter **K** combined with a leaf, heart, or spark

### 3. Ripple Effect

One action → many effects. Expanding circles from a center point.

---

---

## Typography

- **Font:** Inter or Poppins
- **Style:** Bold headings, clean spacing, minimal clutter
- **Feel:** Fintech-clean, not wordy

---

## Logo Style Guidelines

- Flat design (no heavy gradients)
- Works in: app icon, dark mode, light mode
- App icon: Minimal "K" + loop or leaf
- Recognizable at small sizes — not text-heavy

---

## Brand Voice

**Tone:** Simple · Positive · Action-oriented

### Word Replacements

| Instead of | Say |
|------------|-----|
| "Participate in CSR activities" | "Do something good today" |
| "Earn rewards" | "Get rewarded for doing good" |
| "Convert karma to coins" | "Turn your impact into value" |
| "Volunteering opportunities" | "Good deeds near you" |
| "Track your karma points" | "See your impact grow" |

---

## Tagline Options

- **Primary:** "Do Good. Get Rewarded."
- "Good Deeds. Real Rewards."
- "Impact That Pays Back."
- "Turn Good into Value."
- "Do Good. Earn More."

---

## Visual Style

**Use:** Real volunteers, nature, smiles, community gatherings

**Avoid:** Sad or poverty-heavy visuals, overly emotional guilt imagery

**Focus on:** Empowerment, not sympathy

---

## Key Differentiation

This is **NOT:**
- An NGO app
- A donation platform
- A charity tracker

This **IS:**
- Modern and reward-driven
- Lifestyle-integrated
- Gamified with purpose
- Smart fintech-feel

---

## UI Feel

- Clean (fintech-app aesthetic)
- Slight gamification throughout
- Progress indicators for karma level
- Reward highlights with gold/yellow accents
- Level badges (L1–L4) as status symbols
- Coin visuals for earned value

---

## Micro Elements

- 🏅 Badges per event category
- 📊 Level progress bars (L1 → L2 → L3 → L4)
- 🪙 Coin count / earned value
- ✨ Level-up celebrations
- 🔥 Streak indicators

---

## Final Summary for Designer

> Karma is a modern, positive, and rewarding brand that connects real-world good actions with real value. It should feel human, motivating, and trustworthy — not like a charity, but like a smart lifestyle app.
>
> Think: **Headspace meets Robinhood.** Purpose meets reward.
